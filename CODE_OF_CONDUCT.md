# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this community a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex
characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race,
religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of positive behavior include showing empathy, respecting differing
viewpoints, giving constructive feedback, and focusing on what is best for the
community.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discriminatory language, trolling,
personal attacks, and publishing another person's private information.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior privately to `fnpaladini@gmail.com`. Project
maintainers will review and investigate reports promptly and fairly. They will
protect the privacy and security of reporters.

This Code of Conduct adapts the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1:
https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct.html
